HCG Cancer Centre Chinnagadili,Visakhapatnam

OVERVIEW

HCG Cancer Centre, Visakhapatnam, is a leading private comprehensive cancer care facility in Andhra Pradesh, offering advanced and holistic services to cancer patients. With a state-of-the-art 50-bedded hospital, the centre provides a wide range of cutting-edge treatments and diagnostic services, all conveniently located in one place.

Dedicated to delivering value-based and patient-centric cancer care, HCG Visakhapatnam combines clinical expertise, advanced technology, and pathbreaking treatment protocols. Our hospital takes pride in being the first hospital in Andhra Pradesh to house the TrueBeam STx, a leading-edge radiation therapy platform renowned for its precision and treatment effectiveness

The multidisciplinary team at HCG Cancer Centre comprises surgical, radiation, and medical oncologists, along with pain management specialists, onco-dietitians, psycho-oncologists, and general physicians. This collaborative approach ensures that each patient receives a personalised treatment plan tailored to their specific cancer type, stage, and overall condition
